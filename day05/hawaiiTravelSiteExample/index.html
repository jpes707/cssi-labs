Come visit the Rainbow State!

The Hawaiian Islands are easily the most unique place in America, if not the entire world, and that’s not just because it’s a tropical island chain. In addition to its fascinating, yet tumultuous, history and a unique culture that has been shaped and influenced by a variety of peoples over the course of several centuries, Hawaii is the most isolated population center on earth. Traveling to Hawaii is on just about everyone's bucket list. The gorgeous scenery, aloha spirit, stunning beaches, and exciting activities are just a few of the reasons Hawaii is continually rated as a top vacation destination. Plus, it's also the perfect place to relax, unwind and disconnect from your daily routine.

Where to Eat in the Rainbow State
